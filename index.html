Yardman Moto Social Garage

Yardman Moto was born from a single photograph. My grandfather, Albert "Bertie" Petgrave on a motorcycle in 1960s England- leather jacket, windscreen, and a look that said he knew his machine and respected his craft. Before migrating to England in 1959, he was a barber in Jamaica, mastering his trade with precision and pride. After that, he would proceed to spend over two decades working at the C&B factory with the same founding principles that uphold Yardman Moto today: Learn the tools, show up ready, and get the job done right. The motorcycle in the photo cannot be fully identified today, but the spirit is unmistakeable. Today, that legacy continues in a modern way, through a community garage where riders can gather, learn, and work on their bikes together. Yardman Moto exists for people who appreciate real craft. It's more than just a garage, it's a nod to the past and a blueprint for the future. This includes:

  • Membership tiers with tool rental & shop discounts
  • Secure motorcycle storage options
  • Classes: maintenance, safety, customization
  • Events: tech nights, ride-outs, swaps

Roadmap

  1. Phase 1: Build community & merch (live now)
  2. Phase 2: Pop-up wrench nights + classes
  3. Phase 3: Secure space & founding members
  4. Phase 4: Open the social garage
